Welcome!

This is a website for housing rides which can be done virtually in support of Pedaling For Parkinsons, a foundation which supports the research of aerobic excercise as it relates to Parkinson's Disease. To learn more about Pedaling for Parkinsons, click here. To learn about recommended cycling practices, click here.
