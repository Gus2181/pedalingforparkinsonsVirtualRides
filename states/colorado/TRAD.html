T-RAD 2020

Welcome to T-RAD, the Tandem Ride Around Denver!

T-RAD was created by Ken and Judith Wilson, two individuals who are very involved in PFP, and in the greater Parkinson's community. and features a much different format than other rides on this site. Rather than going from checkpoint to checkpoint, we follow Ken and Judith as they ride with Teddy and Freddy, who are pictured to the left. This format also differs in that it is split up into days rather than checkpoints. This means that people do not have quite as much freedom to pace themselves, but have more structure, and know what their daily goal is without needing to determine that for themsevles.

This page also lacks the map and progress bar of other routes on the site. Due to the nature of this ride, it does not make sense to map it out, seeing as it is not one large loop or straight path. The progress bar could make sense, but riders can easily track their progress in the route simply from how many of the days they have completed.

The instructions for each day were originally sent out via email. The ride is being posted here so it can be archived, and accessible to everyone on the internet, not just the recipients of the emails.
