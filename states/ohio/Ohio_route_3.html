Route #3 (326m)

Welcome to the third route in the state of Ohio! Unlike some of the other routes in this state, this travels the entire state, covering an entire 326 miles!

Specifically, this route goes on the Ohio to Erie Trail, a route specifically for non-motorists which travels from Cincinnatti to Lake Erie. These trails are meant for cyclists and hikers, but some trails on the rotue additionally permit equestrian, as well as horse and buggy travel.

This official site for the Ohio to Erie trail (which can be found here) includes several itineraries for those looking to do the route in anywhere from 3 to 8 days. For the purposes of this site, we will be doing the 4 day ride. In total, this route is 323 miles, making it quite long. As a resulg, this specific route may take a fair amount of time to complete (all information on trail courtesy of Wikipedia.com, image courtesy of Kevin Payravi / CC BY-SA)

Checkpoints:

  1. Cincinatti Riverfront (76m to next)
  2. Cedarville (102m to next)
  3. Mount Vernon (78m to next)
  4. Massillon (70m to next)
  5. Cleveland Lakefront (end)
