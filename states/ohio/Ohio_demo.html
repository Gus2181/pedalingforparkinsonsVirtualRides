Ohio Route

This route takes you through some of the towns in the state of Ohio. Among other things, Ohio is famous for having the rock and roll hall of fame, its public universities, namely Ohio State and its football team, and its three major cities: Cleveland, Columbus, and Cincinnatti.

Checkpoints:

  1. Chagrin Falls
  2. Lyndhurst
  3. Cleveland Heights
  4. Shaker Heights
