Ohio

Welcome to the Buckeye State! All of the routes below are in the state of Ohio, home to the Cleveland Clinic and Pedaling For Parkinson's. It also is notable for having the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, the birthplace of Neil Armstrong, the Pro Football Hall of Fame, and much more!. The routes below will take you through towns both large and small all across the state of Ohio. Enjoy (image courtesy of User kla4067 on Flickr / CC BY)!
