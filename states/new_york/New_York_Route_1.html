Route #1 (517.2m)

Welcome! This route will take riders through several of the most noteworthy places in the state of New York. While New York is most famous for New York City, this is only one of the notable places in New York. In addition to the city, the state has a wealth of natural attractions, as well as other cities, and this route will take riders through some of them.

This contains a mixture of some of the cities in New York, as well as some of the natural attractions. It is a very long ride, at 517.2 miles long, with some distances in between checkpoints being nearly 150 miles. This route is intended to take riders a while, and was not desinged as a route where riders will make it to a new checkpoint every day. It may take three or even four days of riding to go some of the longer distances between checkpoints, so remember to pace yourself (image courtesy of MusikAnimal / CC BY-SA).

Checkpoints:

  1. Niagara Falls (81.5m to next)
  2. Rochester (64.5m to next)
  3. Finger Lakes (146m to next)
  4. Cooperstown (84.2m to next)
  5. Catskill Mountains (141m to next)
  6. New York City (end)
