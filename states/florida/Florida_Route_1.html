Route #1 (170.5m)

This route takes riders through a part of florida. Specifically, it goes down the eastern coastline and into the keys. The western coast and interior parts of florida aren't visited in this route.

This route is 170.5 miles long, not extraordinaly long by any means, but it may take a few days for riders to complete. This is all dependent on how comfortable the rider is with biking in general. Someone who has been cycling for a long time may be able to complete this route in a few days, whereas less experienceed riders may take much longer. No matter your level of comfort, make sure to take breaks when you need, pace yourself appropriately, and be safe (image courtesy of I, Jonathan Zander / CC BY-SA).

Checkpoints:

  1. Boca Raton (21.1m to next)
  2. Fort Lauderdale (35.1m to next)
  3. Maimi Beach (74.7m to next)
  4. Key Largo (39.6m to next)
  5. Duck Key (end)
