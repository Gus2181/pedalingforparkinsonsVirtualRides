Route #1 (191m)

This route will take riders on several of the stops on Kentucky's ever famous Bourbon Trail. Kentucky is famous for producing almost all of the bourbon in the united states, and this allows people to see the distilleries in which it gets made. If you get the chance to tour these distilleries in real life, you will also get to meet some of the distillers, and taste a wide variety of bourbons, as well as other whiskeys.

The route itself is 191 miles long, which makes it considerably long, but nothing crazy. It will definitely take multiple days to complete, and may take some people multiple weeks. As with all rides on this site, take your time, be safe, and have fun (image courtesy of Shadle / CC BY-SA)!


Checkpoints

  1. Maker's Mark Distillery (49.5m to next)
  2. Wild Turkey Distillery (11.1m to next)
  3. Woodford Reserve Distillery (67.5.m to next)
  4. Stitzel-Weller Distillery (26.9m to next)
  5. Jim Beam American Stillhouse (36m to end)
