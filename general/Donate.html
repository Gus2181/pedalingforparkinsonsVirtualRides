Every Bit Helps



Every donation goes to helping to fund the research of parkinson's disease, and how aerobic exercise can help to alleviate its symptoms. No matter how large or small a donation is, it helps to further the cause of combatting parkinson's through exercise rather than medication. We thank you for each and every dollar donated.

To make your donation, go to the Pedaling For Parkinson's Donate Page.
