Navigating the Website

Need help navigating? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Here's a list of what the menu options are and what they will take you to:

  • Home: This will simply take you to the homescreen of the website. This simply explains a bit about the site and what's on it, as well as gives you an option to display all of the routes available.
  • About PFP: This will take you to a page explaining some more about Pedaling for Parkinson's, including its founding, mission, and an explanation about why cycling is used to combat Parkinson's
  • Contact Us: This is the place to go if you find yourself needing to talk to someone from Pedaling for Parkinson's. The page will include guidelines on whom to talk to depending on your reason for contacting us.
  • Routes: Hovering over this will give you a dropdown of all of the states with routes on this site. Click on one of them, and click on the route within the state to get riding!
  • More: This contains instructions on how to navigate the website, how to connect with other people in Pedaling for Parkinson's, and how to start a riding group.
  • Donate: This will take you to a page where you can complete a donation via paypal.
